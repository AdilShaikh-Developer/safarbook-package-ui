Amazing Kashmir

Embark on an enchanting 6-day journey through the breathtaking landscapes of Kashmir. Your adventure begins in Srinagar with a warm welcome and a transfer to a traditional houseboat, where you’ll enjoy a serene Shikara ride on Dal Lake. The next day, explore the exquisite Mughal Gardens, including Nishat Bagh and Shalimar Bagh, before heading to Gulmarg for scenic walks, a Gondola ride, and optional skiing. After soaking in Gulmarg's beauty, travel to Pahalgam, visiting Betaab Valley, Chandanwari, and Aru Valley, renowned for their stunning natural vistas. After a leisurely breakfast in Pahalgam, you will return to Srinagar for your departure, cherishing unforgettable memories of your remarkable Kashmir experience.

Day 1

Aurangabad to Delhi

Tue, 19 Nov 2024

Upon arrival in Srinagar, you will be greeted by our representative, who will assist you with your transfer to a traditional houseboat on the picturesque Dal Lake. Upon reaching the houseboat, you will check in and settle into your comfortable, unique accommodation, where you can relax and take in the stunning views. Later in the day, enjoy a 1-hour Shikara ride on the serene waters of Dal Lake. The Shikara ride offers a tranquil way to begin your journey, capturing the essence of Kashmir's natural beauty. After your boat ride, return to the houseboat for a delicious dinner and a peaceful overnight stay. (Dinner)
